QUIZVERSE: PRIVACY POLICY

1. Information We Store

QuizVerse may store certain platform-related information such as usernames, quiz attempts, leaderboard activity, preferences, and learning progress in order to maintain a personalized and interactive experience for users.

2. Purpose Of Data Usage

The information collected is used to improve platform stability, customize quiz experiences, remember user progress, maintain fair gameplay, and enhance overall functionality across different devices and sessions.

3. Browser Technologies

QuizVerse may use browser-based technologies such as cookies, temporary storage, and analytics systems to improve loading speed, remember settings, and better understand how users interact with different sections of the platform.

4. Account & Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help keep user information safe and protected from unauthorized access or misuse. While security systems are regularly maintained, users should also protect their own account credentials and device security.

5. External Links & Services

Some sections of QuizVerse may include links, embedded content, or integrations connected to external platforms. These third-party services operate independently and may follow their own privacy practices and policies.

6. User Control & Preferences

Users may update profile details, manage account preferences, clear saved activity, or modify certain platform settings whenever such options are available within the user interface.

7. Policy Updates

QuizVerse may revise or improve this privacy policy from time to time to reflect platform changes, new features, or updated practices. Continued use of the platform indicates acceptance of the latest version of the policy.
